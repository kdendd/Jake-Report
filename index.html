The plan for May

06
Strategic premise

Make every ad dollar work harder by feeding the team better creative — and grow the channels we don't have to pay for.

Right now, customer acquisition leans heavily on paid media. That works (main campaign at A$47 CPA), but it puts the brand in a position where every customer costs spend and growth depends on creative supply.

The next phase invests in UGC for creative supply, content for organic reach, and finished email flows for retention. By Q3, paid should be one channel among several — not the only one.
